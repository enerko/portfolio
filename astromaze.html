About

Skippy the Swimmer was a group project for a course that I took during my 4th year at University of Toronto. As a team lead and a gameplay programmer of the group, I came up with the game idea, took on the central role for the game design, and programmed fundamental parts of the game, including UI, input, art and audio integration.

Project Information

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Engine: Java

Time spent: 3 months

Team size: 6
