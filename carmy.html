Carmy the Chameleon

About

Carmy the Chameleon is a 2D metroidvania game that follows Carmy, a chameleon who has not yet learned to camouflage, on a journey of discovery through the world. This project started as a game jam, but after the event, a few of us decided to expand it into something bigger. Since then, we've developed a detailed Game Design Document (GDD) and are now focused on releasing a small demo by the end of December as a proof-of-concept. This demo will showcase Carmy's core mechanics, including wall-climbing, grappling, and color- changing, alongside a selection of enemies. Our vision is to create a metroidvania with a strong emphasis on stealth over combat, following Carmy, a chameleon unable to camouflage, as they navigate a dangerous world filled with predators.
