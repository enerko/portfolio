Escape the Dungeon!

About

Escape the Dungeon! was as game built during Nokia 3310 Jam 6, with the theme "FREEZE!". The game has a resolution of 84x48 pixels and 2 colors following the restrictions of the game jam. This is a 2D puzzle game where the player must dodge bullets and reach the stairs on each level to escape the dungeon. The player can achieve this by either pressing a button to freeze the turrets, or by attacking the turrets with a close-range weapon to elimiate the turrets.

Project Info

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Engine: Unity (C#)

Time spent: 10 days

Team size: 2
