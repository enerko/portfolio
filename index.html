About Me

Hi, I'm Rene, a gameplay programmer based in Montreal. I recently graduated with an Honours Bachelor’s Degree from the University of Toronto, where I studied Mathematics and Computer Science. Currently, I work as a QA Tester while pursuing my passion for game development on the side.

Growing up, I was always drawn to art and problem-solving. I loved tackling puzzles from different angles, always seeking innovative solutions. At the same time, I enjoyed creating comics and storybooks to share with family and friends. Thus, discovering game development felt like a perfect fit, as it allowed me to pursue both my artistic creativity and mathematical skills.

What I truly brings me joy is collaborating on projects—working alongside passionate people who share my mindset keeps me motivated. My goal is to one day create my own indie game studio, publishing games that tell captivating stories and inspire others.
