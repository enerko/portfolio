Undying Charm

About

Undying Charm is a game build during the 4 day long game jam, Winter Melon Jam 2023, with the theme "Charm". This is a 2D puzzle platformer where players must use their lamp to "charm" and attract enemies in order to proceed. Players must unlock certain areas that are blocked off by attracting enemies to buttons.

Project Info

Role: Gameplay Programmer

Engine: Unity (C#)

Time spent: 4 days

Team size: 2
